About Us

We're Empower-ED, a free high school admissions support program—for students, by students.

Founded by students at Stuyvesant High School, Empower-ED is an up-and-coming initiative to support middle school students navigate the high school admissions process… for no cost.

About Us

Our Mission

Our Mission

The Empower-ED Mission

At Empower-ED,

At Empower-ED,

At Empower-ED,

We strive to make NYC high school admissions more accessible by providing free, student-led support that empowers communities and closes the opportunity gap.

The NYC admissions process can be overwhelming, from competitive exams, various pathways, and confusing systems. As students who have successfully navigated the system ourselves, we’re here to help your child do the same.

Services

Our Services

  • Admissions Guidance

    Monthly seminars and one-on-one personalized support through the admissions process.

  • Support

    Peer mentorship from students who have been through the process to boost confidence and alleviate stress.

  • Academic Tutoring

    Free SHSAT tutoring and other admission tests, supplemented with study resources and homeworks.

  • Events + Resources

    Monthly newsletters packed with helpful resources, ongoing fundraising for textbooks and study aids, as well as student panels and guest speaker events.
